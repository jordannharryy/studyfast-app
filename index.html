🧬 Why does speed reading actually work?
👁
The bottleneck is your eyes
Your visual cortex processes images in 13ms. The average reader wastes 80% of that capacity through uncontrolled eye movement.
🔁
Regression kills speed
Untrained readers re-read up to 30% of text unconsciously. Breaking this habit alone increases speed by 50%.
🗣
Sub-vocalisation caps you
If you "hear" words as you read, you're limited to speech speed (~300 WPM). Phrase reading bypasses this ceiling entirely.
⚡
Neuroplasticity is fast
The visual system adapts within days of targeted training, not weeks. 15 minutes a day is scientifically sufficient for measurable gains.
